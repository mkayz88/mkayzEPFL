The Kitchen Multi-Timer

You can load meals and the time it takes to cook them. So you can have 2 or 3 timers at the same time when you cook a lot, and not lose any meal because of burning or taking it out early. It may also hold recipes, a "snooze" functions to let it continue a little more.
